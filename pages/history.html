July 22, 2022 - Debut Single "Attention"

NewJeans released their debut single, "Attention," on July 22, 2022. The song quickly topped the charts in South Korea and several other countries. The group also released a music video for "Attention," which was praised for its unique and stylish visuals.

August 1, 2022 - First EP "New Jeans"

NewJeans followed up their debut with the release of their first EP, "New Jeans," on August 1, 2022. The EP was a commercial success, and it debuted at number one on the Gaon Album Chart. The EP also spawned the hit singles "Hype Boy" and "Cookie."

2023 - Second EP "OMG"

In 2023, NewJeans released their second EP, "OMG." The EP was another commercial success, and it debuted at number one on the Gaon Album Chart. The EP also spawned the hit singles "Ditto" and "OMG."
