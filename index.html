NewJeans is a South Korean girl group that debuted on July 22, 2022, under ADOR, a subsidiary of Hybe Corporation.
The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein.

History

NewJeans was first introduced to the public through a series of mysterious teasers that were released in the weeks leading up to their debut.
The teasers featured the members of the group in various settings, and they quickly generated a lot of buzz online.

On July 22, 2022, NewJeans released their debut single, "Attention." The song was an instant hit, and it quickly topped the charts in South Korea and several other countries.
The group also released a music video for "Attention," which was praised for its unique and stylish visuals.

NewJeans followed up their debut with the release of their first EP, "New Jeans," on August 1, 2022.
The EP was a commercial success, and it debuted at number one on the Gaon Album Chart. The EP also spawned the hit singles "Hype Boy" and "Cookie."

In 2023, NewJeans released their second EP, "OMG." The EP was another commercial success, and it debuted at number one on the Gaon Album Chart.
The EP also spawned the hit singles "Ditto" and "OMG."
